Privacy Policy

We design for respect and trust. This policy outlines the data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your information.

Information we collect

We only collect the minimum data necessary to provide and improve our apps.

  • Accountless usage data. Anonymous crash logs and performance metrics help us keep experiences stable.
  • Support conversations. Emails or form submissions you send us are stored securely so we can respond.
  • Opt-in diagnostics. When you explicitly opt in, we record gameplay analytics to tune difficulty curves.

How we use data

Your data powers customer support and app improvements — nothing else.

  • To triage bugs, crashes, or usability issues you report.
  • To measure performance across devices and OS versions.
  • To send release updates if you subscribed to our newsletter.

When we share data

We do not sell or rent your data. Limited sharing occurs only when:

  • Working with infrastructure providers (Apple, GitHub) who process data on our behalf under strict agreements.
  • Complying with legal requests when required by applicable law.

Security & retention

We use encryption in transit and at rest, restrict access to authorized personnel, and review retention quarterly. Support threads older than 18 months are purged unless they remain active.

Your rights

You can request to access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time. Email us at privacy@fera-tech.com with your request.

Policy updates

If we make material changes, we’ll post a changelog here and notify newsletter subscribers.

Contact

For privacy questions, reach out to support@fera-tech.com.
